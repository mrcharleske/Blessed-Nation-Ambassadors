Welcome to Blessed Nation Ambassadors

Founded in 2017 by Lydia Waweru, Blessed Nation Ambassadors is dedicated to transforming lives by empowering youth, supporting farmers and SMEs, sponsoring children living with disabilities, and extending love to street families and the less fortunate. Together, we are building a community of hope and impact.
