Who We Are

Founded by Lydia Waweru in 2017, Blessed Nation Ambassadors is a community-driven initiative serving orphans, widows, street families, youth, farmers and SMEs. We feed, equip, mentor, and walk with families toward a better tomorrow — with special focus on sponsoring children living with disabilities academically and socially.

“Religion that is pure and undefiled before God and the Father is this: to visit orphans and widows in their affliction, and to keep oneself unstained from the world.” — James 1:27
