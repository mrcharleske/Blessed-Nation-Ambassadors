About Us

Blessed Nation Ambassadors is a non-profit in Kenya dedicated to empowering the vulnerable. We support orphans, widows, youth, farmers, SMEs, and persons living with disabilities. Our programs include feeding, outreach, academic sponsorship for children with disabilities, and community empowerment initiatives.

Together, we can build a nation where everyone has dignity, hope, and opportunity.
