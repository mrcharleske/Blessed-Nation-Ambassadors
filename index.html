About Blessed Nation Ambassadors

Founded by Lydia Waweru in 2017, we walk with widows, orphans, street families, children with disabilities, youth, farmers and small business owners — providing food, education sponsorships, mentorship and skills so people can thrive.

“Religion that is pure and undefiled… to visit orphans and widows in their affliction.” — James 1:27
